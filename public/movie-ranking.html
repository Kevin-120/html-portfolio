The Best Series According to Syed

My top 3 Series of all time.

1. Diriliş: Ertuğrul

Diriliş: Ertuğrul, translated as Resurrection: Ertuğrul, is a Turkish historical drama television series inspired by the life of Ertuğrul Bey, the father of Osman I, founder of the Ottoman Empire.

2. Barbaros: Sword of the Mediterranean

Barbaros: Sword of the Mediterranean (Turkish: Barbaroslar: Akdeniz'in Kılıcı) is a Turkish action drama television series created by ES Film, starring Engin Altan Düzyatan and Ulaş Tuna Astepe in the lead roles.

3. Rise of Empires: Ottoman

Rise of Empires: Ottoman is a Turkish historical docudrama, starring Cem Yiğit Üzümoğlu, Tommaso Basili and Daniel Nuță. Its first season, which consists of 6 episodes, is directed by Emre Sahin and written by Kelly McPherson.
